====================================================
PROFIT MILESTONE BELL SYSTEM
============================

Concept:
When users cross major realized profit milestones, the platform triggers a premium institutional stock-market bell sound.

Examples:

* +$500 realized profit
* +$1,000
* +$5,000
* +$10,000
* new all-time highs
* major profit streaks

IMPORTANT:
Do NOT trigger on every trade.

Only on milestone threshold crossings.
