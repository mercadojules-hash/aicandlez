This is a VERY important product clarification.

The TOP 20 LONGS and TOP 20 SHORTS must always feel:
high-confidence,
high-quality,
institutional-grade.

That is critical to the credibility of the platform.

Right now the EVALUATING layer is doing its job —
but the ACTIVE layer is not visually dominating enough.

The user should instantly understand:

“These are the AI’s strongest conviction opportunities right now.”

That means:

1. ACTIVE TOP 20 MUST VISUALLY DOMINATE

The active LONG / SHORT opportunities should have:

* strongest glow
* brightest sparklines
* highest contrast
* clearest typography
* strongest confidence rings
* strongest telemetry energy

These are the “hero” rows of the platform.

2. HIGH CONFIDENCE MUST FEEL IMPORTANT

When confidence is high:
the UI should communicate:
priority,
urgency,
institutional conviction.

Not subtly —
clearly.

Especially:

* 80+
* 90+
* top-ranked opportunities

Those should visually command attention.

3. EVALUATING SHOULD SUPPORT — NOT COMPETE

The evaluating tier is important for:
system intelligence realism.

But:
the ACTIVE layer must remain visually superior.

Current issue:
the evaluating rows are sometimes visually competing too closely with active rows because both live in the same darkness band.

4. RECOMMENDED VISUAL HIERARCHY

ACTIVE TOP SIGNALS:

* vivid neon telemetry
* strongest sparkline luminance
* highest clarity
* richer edge lighting
* more ring energy
* brighter typography
* stronger panel separation

EVALUATING:

* calmer glow
* softer telemetry
* lower saturation
* quieter charts
* reduced ring energy

Still alive —
but clearly secondary.

5. CONFIDENCE LEVELS SHOULD BE EMOTIONAL

The confidence ring should psychologically communicate:
“this is where the AI believes opportunity exists.”

The user should instinctively feel:

* excitement
* focus
* urgency
* conviction

when high-confidence rows appear.

6. TOP 20 SHOULD FEEL ELITE

Not:
“20 random symbols.”

But:
“the 20 strongest opportunities discovered by the AI.”

That distinction matters enormously for perceived intelligence quality.

7. MOST IMPORTANT

Do NOT inflate fake confidence.

The system must remain:
selective,
disciplined,
credible.

The visual hierarchy should amplify:
real conviction —
not fabricate it.
