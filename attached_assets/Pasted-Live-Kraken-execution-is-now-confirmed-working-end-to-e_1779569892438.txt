And to answer your question:

Yes, you can refresh the page.

Refreshing the dashboard should NOT close or lose Kraken positions because the positions live on the exchange/server side, not in the browser UI.

What you MAY lose temporarily:

local UI state
active websocket session
live feed visuals
cached telemetry

But the actual Kraken positions/orders should remain open on Kraken itself.

Since this is still a dev environment with sync issues, wait about 5–10 seconds after refreshing for the telemetry to reconnect before clicking anything else.
