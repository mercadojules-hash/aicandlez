2. PERFORMANCE METRICS VALIDATION
=================================

We need to clearly separate:

* REAL metrics
  vs
* DERIVED metrics

* PLACEHOLDER metrics

* simulated
* seeded
* estimated
* derived from temporary activity

* be labeled appropriately

* High Volatility
* Market Stress

* AI Defensive Mode
* Extreme Volatility

Use terminology aligned with trading intelligence.

===========================

Verify whether:

* win rate
* W/L counts
* profit factor
