* tapping TSLA/META/MSFT/AAPL does not consistently open that symbol’s own detail page

This suggests:

* stale state reuse

Please investigate:

* asset detail routing lifecycle

* useEffect dependencies

* whether the asset page needs a key={symbol} remount strategy
