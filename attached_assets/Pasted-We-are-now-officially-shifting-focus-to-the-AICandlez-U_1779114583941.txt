DESKTOP DASHBOARD LAYOUT STRUCTURE
==================================

## TOP LEVEL (FULL WIDTH):

A full-width live Bloomberg/TradingView-style chart row spanning horizontally across the top of the dashboard.

Requirements:

* multiple live mini charts going across
* animated/live movement
* BTC, ETH, SOL, NVDA, TSLA, SPY etc.
* glowing green/red price movement
* smooth institutional look
* should immediately feel “live”
* charts should NOT look frozen/static
* black backgrounds inside chart containers
* subtle neon glow only (reduce excessive blur/glow)

This top row is the “market heartbeat” of the platform.

==================================================

## SECOND LEVEL:

LEFT SIDE:
ACTIVE POSITIONS PANEL

RIGHT SIDE:
CLOSED POSITIONS / TRADE HISTORY PANEL

These should sit side-by-side on the same row.

ACTIVE POSITIONS PANEL:

* live open trades
* pnl
* entry
* stop loss
* take profit
* AI confidence
* trailing stop status
* live updating
* long + short positions
* ability to pause/close

CLOSED POSITIONS PANEL:

* recently closed trades
* realized pnl
* timestamps
* AI execution summary
* win/loss indicators
* green/red profitability
* scrollable history

Both panels should feel like a hedge fund terminal.
