The execution psychology is working, but the lifecycle timing is too aggressive right now.

Current behavior:

one signal appears
immediately transitions
disappears too quickly
battlefield feels visually empty most of the time

The issue is NOT the scarcity philosophy.

The issue is:
the persistence window is too short for human perception.

A user needs enough dwell time to:

notice the signal
emotionally process it
read the reasoning
understand the AI state transition
feel anticipation

Right now the lifecycle cadence is happening faster than the eye can comfortably absorb.

ADJUSTMENTS NEEDED
EXTEND LIFECYCLE DWELL TIMES

Increase minimum visible duration for:

FORMING
CONFIRMED
EXECUTION READY
WEAKENING

Especially EXECUTION READY.

Recommendation:

FORMING: ~2.5–4s
CONFIRMED: ~4–6s
EXECUTION READY: ~6–10s minimum
WEAKENING: ~3–5s fade/decay

The signal should feel like:
“the AI has conviction”

not:
“a transient notification.”

DO NOT REMOVE SIGNALS IMMEDIATELY AFTER GATE FAILURE

Instead:
transition them into:

WEAKENING
INVALIDATED
CONDITIONS LOST
THESIS BROKEN

Let the battlefield breathe.

A disappearing signal should feel analytical,
not like a rerender.

PRESERVE BATTLEFIELD MEMORY

Even when the AI loses conviction:
the battlefield should retain visual history briefly.

Example:

dimmed card
faded sparkline
ring losing intensity
muted typography

This creates:
continuity + psychological realism.

EXECUTION READY SHOULD DOMINATE ATTENTION

When a signal reaches EXECUTION READY:

keep it alive longer
stronger persistence
stronger visual weight
slower decay

This is the “AI conviction climax.”

KEEP SCARCITY

Do NOT:

increase signal quantity artificially
weaken filters
repopulate with fake opportunities

Scarcity is correct.

The issue is:
signal lifespan and transition pacing.

AI FEED IS EXCELLENT NOW

The AI ACTIVITY feed finally feels:

intelligent
analytical
institutional
alive

Keep building on that.

Especially:

HOLD reasoning
rejection explanations
volatility filters
MTF conflict explanations
position-cap logic

That reasoning layer is becoming one of the strongest parts of the product.

GOAL

The battlefield should feel like:

a live institutional war room
an AI observing market structure continuously
selective high-conviction engagement
persistent analytical memory
disciplined execution

NOT:
a flashing signal board.

The core philosophy is now correct.
Now the timing and persistence need refinement.
