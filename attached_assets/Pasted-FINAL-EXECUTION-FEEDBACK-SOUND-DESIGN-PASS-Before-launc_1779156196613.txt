Before launch, the platform needs premium execution feedback so the AI system feels alive and responsive.

====================================================

1. TRADE START NOTIFICATIONS
   ====================================================

When a trade opens:

* show a subtle animated notification/toast.

Examples:

* “AI LONG EXECUTED — BTC/USD”
* “SHORT POSITION OPENED — TSLA”
* “AI CONFIDENCE 92 — EXECUTION CONFIRMED”
* “POSITION ENTERED — XRP”

The notification should:

* slide/fade smoothly,
* glow subtly,
* auto-dismiss,
* feel institutional,
* NOT look like a gaming popup.

Placement:

* top-right corner preferred,
* stacked intelligently,
* non-intrusive.

==================================

When a trade closes:

* show a premium profit/loss notification.

* “TARGET HIT +4.8%”
* “AI EXIT SUCCESSFUL”
* “PROFIT LOCKED +$284”
* “LONG CLOSED IN PROFIT”

LOSS examples:

* “STOP LOSS TRIGGERED”
* “RISK LIMIT REACHED”
* “POSITION CLOSED -1.4%”

The emotional goal:

* rewarding,
* intelligent,
* confidence-building,
* NOT casino dopamine spam.
