* https://trade.aicandlez.com/portal

are remaining on:
"Resolving session..."
for approximately 10–15 seconds after refresh.

prevent customer/admin flicker.

However:
the gate now appears to be waiting on too many async dependencies before rendering:

DO NOT remove the hydration gate entirely.
The admin/customer flicker fix was correct.
Need staged rendering instead of monolithic blocking.
