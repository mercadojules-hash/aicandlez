Additional UX/Subscription Issues Found During Live Testing

We still have inconsistent trial messaging and incomplete monetization gating across the platform.

Critical Fixes Needed:

1. Trial Messaging Consistency
   The platform currently shows conflicting messaging:

* “14-day free trial”
* “Try free”
* “Paper trading mode — always free”

This is incorrect and inconsistent.

The intended offer is:
✅ 7-Day Free AI Paper Trading Trial

Please standardize this wording across ALL:

* dashboard banners
* pricing page
* profile/settings
* mobile app
* CTA buttons
* onboarding flows
* upgrade modals
* subscription prompts
* locked feature cards

Approved messaging examples:

* “7-Day Free AI Paper Trading Trial”
* “Try AI Paper Trading Free for 7 Days”
* “Upgrade to Unlock Live AI Trading”
* “Paper Trading Active — Live Trading Locked”

2. Remove “14-Day Trial”
   Completely remove all references to:

* “14-day free trial”
* “14 days free”
* “14-day access”

The product is a 7-day paper trading trial, not a 14-day subscription trial.

3. Equities Page Missing Upgrade Gating
   The Equities section currently lacks:

* upgrade banners
* locked premium indicators
* live AI trading prompts
* subscription CTA reinforcement

This creates inconsistent monetization UX.

Add upgrade prompts and premium gating throughout:

* Equities
* Crypto
* Scanner
* Portfolio
* Signals
* Analytics
* Autonomous mode
* Performance intelligence

4. Subscription State UI
   Free/trial users should clearly see:

* LIVE TRADING LOCKED
* PAPER TRADING ACTIVE
* Upgrade Required
* Days Remaining
* Premium Features Locked

The platform should never feel fully unlocked for free-tier users.

5. CTA Standardization
   Replace vague wording like:

* “Try free”

With clearer conversion-focused wording:

* “Start 7-Day AI Trial”
* “Unlock Live AI Trading”
* “Upgrade to Trade Real Funds”
* “Activate AI Live Mode”

6. Mobile + Desktop Audit
   Please audit all pages/screens on:

* desktop dashboard
* mobile responsive web
* Expo mobile app

to ensure monetization messaging, subscription gating, and trial wording are fully consistent platform-wide.

Please provide screenshots after the monetization/gating consistency pass is complete.
